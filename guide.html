Guide

1. Set Clear Goals:
  • Define your short-term and long-term goals to create a sense of purpose and direction.
  • Break down larger goals into smaller, actionable tasks for better clarity and focus.
2. Prioritize Tasks:
  • Evaluate tasks based on urgency and importance using techniques like the Eisenhower Matrix.
  • Prioritize high-priority tasks and tackle them first to prevent procrastination.
